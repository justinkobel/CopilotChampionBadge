Show off your Copilot Champion Status

Upload your Microsoft 365 profile image, and we'll add a Copilot Champion badge to it.

Follow the steps below to get your new profile image:

  • Download your current Microsoft profile image from your profile (or maybe this is the perfect time to use a new one)
  • Upload your Microsoft 365 profile image here:
  • Preview your image and make sure it looks like you want it to - you can drag/drop the badge if you would like
  • Download your profile image, now with a Copilot Champion badge:
  • Go back to your your profile, upload your new image, and let the world know you're a Copilot Champion!

This solution is not KiZAN supported, but we are happy to help you with your Microsoft 365 Copilot journey. Learn more about how KiZAN can enable your users as Copilot Champions.
